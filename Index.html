Welcome To Talenta Agro Crops

The Talenta Coco Coir Products has been operating for more than five years in the distribution of Coco Peat & coir products for the Agricultural and Horticultural, serving producers of substrates and large consumers all over the world.

Talenta Coco Coir Products one of the leading manufacturers, exporters and suppliers of cocopeat & coir products from Kurunegala, Sri Lanka. We have taken to a new markets for supplies and to bring new resources to its customers.

Talenta Coco distributes products resulting from the processing of coconut husk into Coconut Fiber & CocoPeat products for the use in Agriculture, Horticulture as well as in animal husbandry. Talenta Coco ensures & aims at consistent quality products over time.

We are specialising in

  • Coco Peat Blocks
  • Coir Peat Bricks
  • Coco Coins & Discs
  • Coco Weed Mats
  • Coco Peat Grow Bags
  • Curled/Twisted Coir Ropes
  • Coco Coir Bales

About Talenta Agro Crops
